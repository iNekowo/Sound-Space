Whether you're looking for a rhythm game as a full fledged gamer or as a casual gamer, Sound Space has the perfect unique game experience for you!

What is Sound Space?

Sound Space is a rhythm game in Roblox where you hit notes coming towards you on a track in unique spaces with a variety of meshes and colors. Created by TominoCZ and Neko_Mao!

What makes it unique?

Challenge more than 1500 different maps in a 3x3 grid with a diversity of block meshes, colors, spaces and customizable cursors.

Map Editor

Looking forward to playing a custom map? You can make your own one in the Sound Space Map Editor.

Community Corners

Here you can find community provided maps and more in the Community Spreadsheet and the Skill and Map Leaderboard.
